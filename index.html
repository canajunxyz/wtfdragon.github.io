Why Cheese?

Truthfully, there's not going to be a lot that gets said here on the topic of cheese.

There's a photo of cheese, at any rate. Enjoy.

Actually, I can't even take credit for the name; there was a blog back in the early 2000s that was called With Cheese, which aggregated a lot of eclectic content and was a decent read during its heyday.

This isn't some attempt to re-create that sort of thing, though. But the name was (kind of) available, and it's catchy in a weird sort of way.
